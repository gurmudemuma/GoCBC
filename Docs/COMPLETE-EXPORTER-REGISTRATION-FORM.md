## ✅ **Form Validation Rules**

1. All required fields must be filled
2. TIN must be 10 digits
3. Email must be valid format
4. Phone numbers must be valid Ethiopian format
5. Dates must be in YYYY-MM-DD format
6. License expiry must be future date
7. Capital amount must meet minimum (15M ETB for exporters)
8. At least one export market must be selected
9. At least one sourcing region must be selected
10. Professional taster certificate must not be expired
